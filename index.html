A BEACH FUNDRAISER...

A day full of sipping coconuts (from reusable straws cause we care about our turtle friends), surfing, singing and dancing that results in the ocean being saved. Yes, you read that right. This fundraiser is going to put in every penny it collects into saving the oceans and cleaning the beaches so that our sea life friends get to live longer and better lives.

The complete donation collected at this fundraiser will be spent cleaning the beaches and saving marine life. It's time we save our planet from the effects of pollution that we have been causing for so many years.
